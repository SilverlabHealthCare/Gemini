Food Poisoning Management

Schedule 0 (Open Shop) Retail Accessible
  • ● Rehidrat: The legacy market leader for oral rehydration. Key for preventing dehydration during vomiting/diarrhea.
  • ● Activated Charcoal: A natural absorbent used to neutralize toxins in the digestive tract.
Schedule 1 (Pharmacy Only) Expert Recommendation
  • ● Tasectan (Gelatin Tannate): #1 doctor-prescribed diarrhea treatment. Forms a protective film against pathogen adhesion.
  • ● Smecta (Diosmectite): Natural clay-based binder for viruses and toxins. High pediatric demand.
🤒

Stomach Flu (Viral) Care

Schedule 0 (Open Shop) Retail Accessible
  • ● Buscopan: Frequently purchased to manage painful abdominal cramps associated with viral bugs.
  • ● Paracetamol: Standard first-line treatment for accompanying fever and body aches.
Schedule 1 (Pharmacy Only) Expert Recommendation
  • ● Imodium (Loperamide): High-volume standard for rapid motility relief. Recommended for short-term use.
  • ● Reuterina: Top-selling probiotic for restoring flora balance post-viral infection.
