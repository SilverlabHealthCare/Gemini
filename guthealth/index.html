The Paradigm Shift: From Vitamin C to Probiotics

Historically, the SA market relied heavily on generic Vitamin C and Zinc supplements for immunity. However, recent search trends and sales data indicate a massive pivot. Consumers are increasingly proactive about gut flora, viewing it as the primary defense against systemic illness and localized stomach bugs (like rotavirus or food-borne pathogens).

The OTC Arsenal

When struck by food poisoning or gastroenteritis, South Africans turn to accessible over-the-counter (Schedule 0-2) solutions. Rehydration remains the paramount priority, followed by acute symptom control. Probiotics are increasingly purchased concurrently for recovery.

Purchase Drivers

Unlike daily supplements, purchasing behavior during acute gastric distress is highly reactive. Speed of relief dominates consumer priorities, often outweighing price sensitivity or brand loyalty during severe stomach bugs.
