The Disposable Income Gap

Real disposable income per capita has remained largely stagnant or declined for most South Africans over the last 5 years. While inflation has stabilized, the high cost of debt and utilities means "true" discretionary income is at a decade low.

The "Healthcare-At-Home" Boom
